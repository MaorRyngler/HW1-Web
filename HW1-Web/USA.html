The United States, a vast and diverse country in North America, is known for its significant cultural, economic, and political influence globally. With iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, the Grand Canyon, and the White House, the U.S. is a melting pot of cultures and traditions. Home to over 330 million people, it boasts a dynamic economy, innovative technology sector, and a rich history of democracy and social progress.
